Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Welcome to Haven ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have control over your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication details.
  • Property Data: Addresses, lease dates, and rent amounts you enter.
  • Documents & Media: Leases, notices, photos, and videos you upload to the App.
  • Communications: Text logs of chats with the AI Assistant and maintenance records.

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (for crash reporting and analytics).
  • Usage Data: Interactions with features (e.g., screens visited, buttons pressed) to help us improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to provide and improve the Haven service:

  • Core Functionality: Storing your documents and enabling AI features (like lease analysis).
  • AI Processing: We use third-party LLMs (Large Language Models) to process text. We do not use your data to train public AI models.
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and fix bugs.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256) at rest and TLS during transmission. Some data may be cached locally on your device for performance. You can clear this in Settings.

We implement strict access controls. Our employees do not access your private documents unless explicitly authorized by you for support purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase) and AI providers (OpenAI/Anthropic) strictly for processing.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by a valid subpoena or court order.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access (export) all your data via the "Export" feature in the App, or permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings > Delete Account.

6. Children's Privacy

Haven is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect specific personal information from children.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at:
support@havenapp.com
